\# FORECAST AI



\## Project Overview



FORECAST AI is an AI-powered financial planning platform designed to help users manage salaries, expenses, savings, and tax compliance.



The system analyzes historical financial data and provides predictive insights that help users make informed financial decisions.



\---



\## Problem Statement



Many individuals struggle to:



\- Track monthly expenses

\- Predict future spending

\- Align expenses with salary

\- Understand tax liabilities

\- Plan savings effectively



Existing tools provide tracking but lack intelligent forecasting and tax optimization guidance.



\---



\## Proposed Solution



FORECAST AI will:



\- Track salary information

\- Track expenses

\- Predict future expenses using AI

\- Forecast future salary growth

\- Calculate tax liabilities

\- Suggest tax-saving opportunities

\- Generate financial reports

\- Provide personalized insights



\---



\## Target Users



\### Individual Employees



People who receive monthly salaries and want better financial planning.



\### Freelancers



Individuals with varying monthly income.



\### Small Business Owners



Users who need expense forecasting and tax planning.
